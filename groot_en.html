Account Deletion Guide

Touch the settings icon on the home screen to navigate to the 'Other Settings' menu.
You can delete your account via the 'Delete Account' menu at the bottom right corner of that page.

What happens when you delete your account?

All information saved so far will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.
It may take up to 2-3 days for the account deletion to be completed after submitting the deletion request.

Even if you delete your account, the premium subscription will not be automatically canceled.

Subscription-related matters (cancellation/refund) must be managed directly through the Play Store app.

Even if you delete your account, you can always create a new one.
