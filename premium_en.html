■ Unlimitied download of driving report PDF

Download unlimited driving record PDFs.

Download the driving record as a file from the statistics page.
You can create a file by selecting the desired period and contents to be displayed.


Downloaded PDF files can be stored on your smartphone or computer.
You can also print out the driving log and keep it.

Keep driving records in various ways.


■ View earning and expenses in charts

Analyze your earnings at a glance with charts.

You can analyze the earnings status easily and quickly by visualizing it.



■ Use of statistical graph

Use the statistical graph to check the trend of driving records.

It shows driving records subdivided by last 7 days, month, and year.
It is organized by item, so you can easily analyze driving patterns.



■ Premium cargo log(for Cargo/Dump)

Provides additional input items required for cargo transportation.

Additional items can be entered in addition to the basic input items.


You can create your own customized cargo log with only the desired input items.

Create a cargo log by entering useful information when transporting cargo.
