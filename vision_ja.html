Atelier for kids

It is an application that keeps children's works, and provides an individual work exhibition space.




APP SCREEN




Upload kid's artwork

You can take a picture of your child's work and register it right away. You can also choose a frame that matches the artwork.


Manage kid's artworks

You can manage the work of multiple children. In addition, each child can categorize and store the works of their choice.


Look at other children's works

Aren't you curious about what other children create? Enjoy and communicate with other friends' work.

PDF DOWNLOAD


Make a great portfolio with your kid's art!

Download the PDF for free using the template provided by Kitelier.

You can create a child's work album easily and simply without wasting time and money.
You can also keep the files on your computer or private storage or send them to your family.
